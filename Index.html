ABOUT US

Portrait Interiors was established in 2009 to serve the interiors industry with high quality distinguished products, we rely on our extensive experience in design, engineering and contracting to provide utmost satisfaction to our clients. We strive to provide our clients with maximum functionality, quality and price.
Being a Contract Interior specialist, we provide the full range in interior jobs, from design, supervision, supply and on site construction. Our teams of professional craftsmen are highly trained to provide the highest quality achievable. Our disciplines are diversified including residential, commercial and hospitality; our CV is full of successfully completed projects and satisfied clients.

Company Profile

SERVICES

Contract Interior

Contract Interior is a term used to describe a job from A to Z, from Concept Design to final handing over, i.e. Turn Key Contract. This is a specialty of the few, it involves huge coordination efforts between multi-disciplinary parties, and a strict commitment to the principles of Modern Project Management. 

We provide this service utilizing our experience and in-house disciplines along with outsourcing the parts of work that cannot be produced in-house. Hospitality Sector Contract Interior is our main stream in this specialty, along with other sectors like residential, corporate and commercial. We  are a major supplier of many international manufacturers in this field.
