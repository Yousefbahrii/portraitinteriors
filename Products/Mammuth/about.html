About Us

MAMMUTH is one of the world leading manufacturers of Wood Veneer flooring systems.

Wood Veneer flooring gives an edge over the laminated flooring by providing the natural beauty of wood grains, no plank is similar to another, each and every piece has its own distinctive look and texture, contrary to laminated flooring that has 4 or 5 patterns that are repeated tediously in the floor.

Know How

Wood Veneer flooring requires a special know how in manufacturing due to the limited thickness of the natural veneer of 0.6mm. Special care and finishing is required to produce a durable floor that can withstand tear, wear and scratching.

The system is available in a wide renage of colors and textures, and can be based on MDF or Plywood panels depending on the intensity of use and price range.
