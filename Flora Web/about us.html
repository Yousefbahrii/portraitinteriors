about flora wooden floors

 

flora wooden floors is a premier brand in the provision of solid wood flooring solutions, we design, manufacture and install customized wood flooring to specific client's requirements.

flora wooden floors was established in the year 2009 by Core International Trading & Investment Co. in Amman, Jordan with a vision of becoming a major supplier for wood flooring systems and solutions in the Middle East.

All design activities are made in house, whilst manufacturing activities are done in Cairo, Egypt; being a focal point in the region with high and extensive technical experience in the field of wood flooring.
