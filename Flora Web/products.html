products

 

flora wooden floors specializes in the production of Engineered wood flooring systems, these systems provide the optimum solution for solid wood flooring.

The system is made up from multilayers of solid wood and plywood material to a total thickness of 12mm, finished with a special type of laquer paint.

Several types of wood are utilized in the flooring system including Oak, Mahogany, Teak, Cherry and Walnut.

Different categories of wood flooring solutions are available, from the standard parquet floor to the patterned tiles with border lines and decorative art work.
